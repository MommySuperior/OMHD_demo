Commissioner Dr. Ann Sullivan visited the Staten Island Borough President’s Office to discuss the FY 2027 Executive Budget proposal and the strong investments to expand access to mental health care statewide that are being made under the leadership of Governor Hochul. Commissioner Sullivan also joined members of the Staten Island Suicide Prevention Task Force to discuss some of the initiatives, including the MISSION Project, a multi-year effort to assess and provide life-saving services to at-risk youth and young adults living in the borough.
