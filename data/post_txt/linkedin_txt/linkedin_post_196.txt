I’m wearing green this month for Mental Health Awareness Month because it’s a good reminder to check in on each other.

Each year in the U.S., 20% of adults and 17% of adolescents experience a mental illness, and those needs continue to grow in both complexity and urgency. At HealthPartners, we are focused on closing the gap between demand and access.

One way we are doing that is through a rapid access model that brings behavioral health expertise directly into primary and specialty care visits. When we can address needs earlier, we reduce barriers for patients, shorten wait times for ongoing therapy, and improve access across the system.

We’re also addressing stigma, which remains one of the most persistent barriers to care. Through our Make It OK campaign, we’re helping normalize conversations about mental health and supporting more open dialogue in our communities.

Help us end the stigma: https://makeitok.org/

Our commitment to supporting mental wellness for those we serve is a priority year-round, not just in May.
