This Mental Health Awareness Month, we’re highlighting how well-being can thrive in the workplace. Behavioral health remains one of the leading causes of disability, underscoring the challenges many workers navigate every day.* 

We partner with organizations in our communities that expand access to mental and behavioral health resources to address the many factors that influence overall health and well-being.

That same commitment extends to our employees, with a wide range of accessible benefits and resources designed to support the whole person. Our holistic approach has earned Unum Group recognition from Business Group on Health as a Best Employer for Excellence in Health and Well-being for seven consecutive years. 

Mental health shows up in real moments, at work and beyond, and when we support one another, we create stronger, healthier communities for all. 


*Unum Group internal claims data, 2025
