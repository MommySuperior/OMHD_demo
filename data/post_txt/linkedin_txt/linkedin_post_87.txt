As the community mental health sector continues to grow and evolve, traineeships offer a practical pathway to build workforce capability and support future workforce needs. 

Join our upcoming Workforce development in practice webinar, where we will explore peer work training pathways in NSW, the benefits and requirements of traineeships, and the practical supports available to organisations, supervisors and learners.

This webinar is open to the broader community mental health sector, providing an opportunity for both MHCC members and non-members to learn more about workforce development pathways and available supports.  

When: Monday 15 June, 10:00am to 11:15am 
Where: Online via Zoom

Hosted by our CEO Dr Evelyne Tadros, we'll hear from our speakers, Michelle Hoomans , Learning and Development Manager, Mental Health Coordinating Council, Jessica Radican (English), Principal Statewide Peer (Lived Experience) Workforce Officer, Mental Health Branch, NSW Ministry of Health, Susan Scowcroft, Executive Director, NSW Community Services and Health Industry Training Advisory Body, and Leon Esho, Training Advisor, Training Services, NSW Department of Education.
