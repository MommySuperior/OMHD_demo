Integrating mental health into primary care is good medicine that improves outcomes and reduces costs.
 
I’m proud to co-author on this new op-ed with Tom Wroth, MD, MPH and Christoph Diasio, MD FAAP. Together, we explore how a more coordinated, whole-person approach can expand access to care, catch issues earlier, and ultimately save lives.
 
I’m even more excited about our work together to reward practices that bring mental health into the pediatric setting. The innovative collaboration between Blue Cross NC and Sandhills Pediatrics will help care teams identify concerns during routine visits, connect patients to the appropriate level of support, and coordinate care over time. This approach helps families receive care in a trusted setting and will reduce reliance on emergency and other high-cost interventions.
