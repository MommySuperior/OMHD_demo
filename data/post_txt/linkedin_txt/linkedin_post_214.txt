Africans for Mental Health (AFMH), in partnership with Sheppard Pratt, is pleased to offer FREE virtual trainings in Adult Mental Health First Aid (MHFA) in 2026.

These trainings are open to family caregivers, community members, and adults (18+) who want to gain the skills to recognize, respond to, and support individuals experiencing mental health challenges or a suicide crisis.

Training Format:
2 hours of self-paced online pre-work
6 hours of live, instructor-led training delivered over two days (3-hour sessions each day)

Participants must have a working camera and microphone and remain on camera during the sessions (except during scheduled breaks)

Participants who complete the training will receive a 3-year Mental Health First Aider certification.

What You’ll Learn

How to recognize signs and symptoms of mental health and substance use challenges

How to support adults experiencing distress

How to connect individuals to appropriate professional resources
