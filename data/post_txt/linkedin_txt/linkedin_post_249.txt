🧠Last week was Mental Health Awareness Week. To mark this, we hosted a range of activities designed to support wellbeing, encourage open conversations, and bring colleagues together.

🌱On Tuesday, we had Jen Davis from Flourish Nutrition come in to speak about the benefits that good nutrition can have on your cognitive function and mental health. The theme of MHAW this year is take action, so we wanted to give colleagues actionable insights into how best to support their mental health and wellbeing.

🍰On Wednesday, things got competitive when we hosted the Great Dockyard Bake Off. Bakers competed for the crown of Best Baker on Base and they did not disappoint! Congrats to Billy Bulloch‘s daughter Scar for claiming victory with an excellent Sandy Surprise Cake! We are proud to say that the proceeds of selling the bakes raised over £500 for Solent Mind.

🥾 Thursday saw KBS colleagues go on a mental health walk with Solent Original Walks around Old Portsmouth. It was a great opportunity to boost our knowledge of Portsmouth’s history, uncover hidden gems and hear underrated tales of Portsmouth’s past with our brilliant guide John whilst encouraging important conversations about mental health.

🌟Thank you to everyone who’s help and support helped make this week happen!
