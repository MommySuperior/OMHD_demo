One of the clearest emerging messages is that young people’s mental health, participation in education and employment, social protection, life opportunities and future resilience cannot be addressed in isolation from one another.

The findings also reinforce the urgent need for earlier intervention and prevention, alongside more integrated and practical support systems that help young people experiencing mental ill health remain connected to learning, participation and decent work.
