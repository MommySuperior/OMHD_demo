🧠📱 How Reddit posts reveal a major shift in mental health conversations.

#UniMelb researchers Jemima Kang, A/Prof Mike Conway, and Prof Nick Haslam analysed over 14 million Reddit posts and comments to understand how social media shapes our understanding of mental health.

The findings highlight the growing influence of social media in shaping how people understand, discuss and seek support for mental health.
