Strong governance supports strong outcomes for young people.

At Parkville Youth Mental Health and Wellbeing Service, our board plays a critical role in shaping safe, effective and accountable care.

Our nine independent non‑executive directors bring deep expertise across youth mental health, governance and service delivery. Importantly, our board includes people with lived experience of mental health challenges, alongside family members and carers.

These perspectives strengthen decision‑making and keep our strategy grounded in the real experiences of navigating the mental health system.

Together, our board provides clear oversight and values‑led leadership. Their guidance supports our delivery of evidence‑based, trauma‑informed care and drives continuous improvement across the service system - always centred on the needs of young people.
