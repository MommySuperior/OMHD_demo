On this World Mental Health Day we want to take a moment to share resources and raise awareness about the importance of this topic. Our individual mental health plays a huge role in our emotional, physical, and social wellbeing and impacts how we operate day to day. Our officers respond to calls for service involving mental health concerns many times a day, stabilizing crisis calls and providing much needed compassion and care. Our Behavioral Health Response Team (BHRT) consists of two sworn Behavioral Health Officers and two civilian Mental Health Coordinators who review all mental health crisis calls that officers respond to and determine which cases require additional follow up. BHRT will coordinate with family members and mental health community partners to connect people with service and reduce future law enforcement interventions. If you are struggling with mental health, know that you are not alone.
