A reminder that in mental health settings (though evidence appears to support RCA's in general health settings) the use of root cause analysis processes doesn't have a lot of evidence.

💠 There's evidence that the RCA recommendations aren't taken up much by clinicians often
💠 There's evidence that even when implemented the recommendations don't necessarily lead to improvements in safety and quality
💠 The model has tensions with lived expertise and being grounded in epistemic justice
💠 The model usually fails to comply with existing legal obligations in the Victorian Healthcare system by requiring providers to properly consider and comply with relevant human rights in process, recommendations and ultimate actions.

The model may be modifiable, however these limitations should be noted in mental health settings.
