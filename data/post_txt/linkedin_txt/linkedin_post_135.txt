Mental Health Awareness Month is an important reminder that well-being has to be part of how we think about the way people work and grow.

People bring their full lives with them into the workplace. Stress, caregiving, burnout, grief and uncertainty do not stay neatly outside the office door.

That is why supportive cultures matter. When people feel safe enough to ask for help and supported enough to say what they need, they are better able to show up fully in their work. Mental health is personal, but supporting it is something we all have a role in. #MentalHealthAwarenessMonth #Wellbeing
