Pride Month is a time to recognize and support LGBTQ+ communities while also acknowledging the importance of spaces where people can feel safe being themselves.

In mental health work, that includes creating environments where people are treated with respect, listened to without judgment, and able to access support that reflects their experiences.

Everyone deserves care, community, and the ability to show up as themselves.

From MHALA, Happy Pride Month. 💙💛🏳️‍🌈
