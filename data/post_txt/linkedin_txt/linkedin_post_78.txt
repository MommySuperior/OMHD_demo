We are very excited to announce the launch of the Construction Mental Health Alliance (CMHA).  The Alliance Partners are focused on providing to the Construction Industry the most relevant, user friendly and concise information regarding head injury prevention, opioid misuse and better pain management, mental health and suicide prevention, fatigue management and wellness.  We realize it can be overwhelming to find the information that you need, quickly, efficiently and effectively.  Our goal is to provide you the information you need, when you need it.  For the construction industry by the construction industry.
Thank you to our Sponsoring Organization; American Society of Concrete Contractors (ASCC), Supporting Organizations; AGC, Safe Project and Missouri Works and Supporting Companies; Baker Concrete, Structural Group, Jordan Foster and Alliance Safety Council.
