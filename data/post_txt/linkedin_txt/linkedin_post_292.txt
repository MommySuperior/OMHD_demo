Today on World Mental Health Day, we’re reminded that everyone is affected by mental health, whether personally or through a family member or friend. Companies can lead in breaking down stigma, not just with words, but with action—a clear purpose, a defined program and ways for people to participate. 
 
For example, 22,000 Accenture people around the world have been trained as mental health allies to support their colleagues, which helps people have the confidence to ask for what they need to thrive, such as accommodations and access to mental health services. And our Well-being Hub offers our people and their families a single online source for mental, physical and financial health resources.
 
Mental health is everyone's concern. With actions like the ones we're taking at Accenture, businesses can help their people and families build resilience and create more belonging and understanding—this is something we all need to do together.
