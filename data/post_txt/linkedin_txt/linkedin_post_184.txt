Mental Health Month is a reminder that performance is not only driven by skills and targets — it is driven by people.
In today’s workplace, mental health is not a “nice to have.” It is a business priority. Teams that feel safe, supported, and heard are the ones that sustain performance, innovation, and resilience.
As leaders, our role is clear:
Create environments where people can speak, recover, and perform at their best.
Because behind every mission , there is a human being.
