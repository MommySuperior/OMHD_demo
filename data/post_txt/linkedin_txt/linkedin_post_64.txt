It was a pleasure to take part in Black Dog Institute’s Expert Insights for Health Professionals series with Kylie Maidment, discussing #AI in mental health care.

I appreciated the opportunity to talk about digital phenotyping, including how data from everyday devices could help us better understand changes in mental health, improve treatment timing, and support more personalised care.

We also covered why health professionals should be paying attention to these developments, including the opportunities, limitations, and practical implications for clinical care.
