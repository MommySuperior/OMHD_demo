Mental Health Awareness Month prompts meaningful conversations about the human side of medicine. 
 
At Teva, our work goes beyond medicines alone. We work to understand the everyday realities of living with mental illness and how we can best support people living with #schizophrenia, #HuntingtonsDisease (HD), and #TardiveDyskinesia (TD) along what can be a challenging journey. That’s why I’m proud of the recent launch of Home Ground™ Schizophrenia Community, a new online platform designed by and for people living with schizophrenia and their care partners.
 
Initiatives like Home Ground reflect our commitment to supporting patients through diagnosis, treatment, adherence, symptom management, and day-today living by connecting people to education, resources, and community, to a place that can make navigating care feel more accessible and less isolating.
 
My heartfelt gratitude goes out to our incredible teams that continue to keep patients at the center of everything we do, because we are all in for better health.
