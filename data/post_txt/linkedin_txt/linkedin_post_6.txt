Strengthening Mental Health, Resilience, and Belonging for Adolescent Boys

I thrilled that my team at the Mount Sinai Institute for Health Equity Research (IHER) officially launched our youth mental wellness & resilience program at the Flatbush YMCA of Greater New York.

Brothers=Connect is a more than a project.

It's a cultural intervention.

We aim to transform mental health among boys by infusing principles about purpose, balance, kinship, and guidance to strengthen social connectedness, reduce help seeking stigma, and reduce depressive & anxiety symptoms.

Grateful to partner with leaders like Drs. Peter Wyman and Sherry Molock and our entire team focused on saving lives through sports.

Excited to expand Brothers=Connect with MADE Hoops this summer and beyond!
