May is Mental Health Awareness Month in the U.S., and this year’s theme – More Good Days, Together – is a meaningful reminder that well-being isn’t something we manage alone. It’s shaped by how we show up for one another and the support systems we put in place. At Medline, we provide our teams with resources that fit into real, busy lives – helping employees manage stress, build resilience, and stay at their best both at work and outside of it. Well-being looks different for everyone, and progress doesn’t have to be perfect. What matters is creating an environment where people feel supported and can do their best work.
