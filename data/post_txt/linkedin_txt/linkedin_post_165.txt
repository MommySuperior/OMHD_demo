May is Mental Health Awareness Month!

This month, the Commission reaffirms our commitment to accessible, compassionate behavioral health support.

If you or someone you know is experiencing a behavioral health or substance use crisis, help is available 24/7 by calling or texting 988, the Suicide & Crisis Lifeline.

The Commission encourages all Californians to learn about 988, share this resource widely, and help reduce the stigma around seeking support.
