What an honor and a privilege it was to be a part of an incredible team at HRSA to support the Pediatric Mental Healthcare Access (PMHCA) Program! Pediatric mental health needs are soaring, and this program works to meet that need quickly by equipping primary care providers and other child-serving sectors with psychiatric consultations, training, resources, and direct services to patients. I continue to be inspired by the talented leaders at the state, territory and tribal programs who devote themselves to this program day in and out!
