Mental health is health. 💙 

This Mental Health Awareness Month, we're proud to shine a spotlight on the vital role mental health plays in overall health and well-being — and to celebrate our dedicated mental health care physicians and professionals who deliver high-quality, evidence-based care to our patients every day.

Their work is making a difference: For seven consecutive years, Kaiser Permanente Northern California has been designated a best-rated health plan for behavioral and mental health care by California's Center for Data Insights and Innovation. This also marks the seventh year in a row that our California plans have been the only 5-out-of-5-star ranking recipients in the state. ⭐ 

Behind that recognition is real investment and growth: Kaiser Permanente has invested more than $2 billion since 2020 to expand mental health facilities and train and license new clinicians across the organization, and increased our mental health workforce in Northern California by 25%. And through our Kaiser Permanente Northern California Mental Health Training Collaborative, the largest non-governmental mental health training program in the U.S., we're training 600 aspiring therapists and psychologists this year alone.

To all our mental health care providers: Thank you for showing up for patients, families, and members across Northern California. Your commitment to compassionate whole-person care is at the heart of what we do. 🙏
