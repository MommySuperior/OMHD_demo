The NYC Mayor's Office of Community Mental Health (OCMH) Executive Director Eva Wong spoke with Crain's New York Business health reporter Ethan Geringer-Sameth about the City’s evolving approach to mental health care that centers health-focused, community-based responses to crises while connecting #NYC to the right kind of care.

Wong underscores the need to strengthen and scale the behavioral health workforce amid an ongoing shortage, while emphasizing the importance of reducing #stigma that can limit access to mental health care.
