May is Mental Health Awareness Month, and a good reminder that not all struggles are seen or heard. Our veterinary community navigates challenges that their patients, clients, or even friends and family may never know. Things like compassion fatigue and burnout can take a real toll. Caring for animals is a labor of love that draws an exceptional type of person. We as an industry must continue to step up and protect their well-being.

NAVC is focused on expanding our resources in this area to ensure our community has what they need to thrive. Other organizations like Project Sticker and Not One More Vet are doing important work to support veterinary professionals and advocate on their behalf. Together, we can foster a healthier, more compassionate future for everyone in veterinary medicine.
