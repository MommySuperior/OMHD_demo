The Mental Health Commission, through the Decision Support Service, has launched a public information campaign about Advance Planning.

Any adult, at some point, may become unable to make decisions for themselves. Advance planning ensures your personal choices are known and upheld, should this happen.

Advance planning lets you record your wishes about your treatment, your welfare and what happens with your money and property.

Look out for our advertisements featuring real couples and families - including Tom & Linda, Ro & John, Noel & Alan, and Terri & John - across radio, TV and online this summer.

Share the message with your loved ones. Advance planning is for everyone, at any time.
