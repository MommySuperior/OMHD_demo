To mark Mental Health Awareness Week, we share key learning from our patient safety investigations into mental health – focusing on how a system based, compassionate approach can reduce harm and support patients, families and NHS staff.

The blog, written by Philippa Styles, highlights:
- Why patient safety and psychological safety go hand in hand.
- How fear of blame can block learning after harm.
- Why most safety issues are system problems, not individual failings.
- What a fair, trauma informed response looks like in practice.

This learning will resonate with organisations working under the Patient Safety Incident Response Framework (PSIRF), and anyone committed to improving safety culture in mental health care.
