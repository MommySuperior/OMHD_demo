Mental Health Awareness Month in the U.S. and Mental Health Awareness Week in the U.K. are important reminders that supporting well-being must be an ongoing commitment.
 
Creating environments where people feel supported, connected, and empowered to prioritize their well-being helps individuals and teams thrive. From emotional and social well-being to career and financial support, meaningful resources and open conversations can make a lasting impact.
 
This month and beyond, let’s continue encouraging conversations around mental health and reminding one another that seeking support is a sign of strength.
