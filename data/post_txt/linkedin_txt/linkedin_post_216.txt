#MentalHealthAwarenessMonth is a powerful reminder that behind every statistic is a young person navigating challenges no one should face alone.
 
I’ve been thinking about the moments—and the people—that can change the trajectory of a child’s life. A trusted adult. A safe space. Someone who sees them.
 
That’s what makes our partnership with Boys & Girls Clubs of America so meaningful. Together, we’re equipping Club staff with the tools to recognize and respond to trauma, helping young people build resilience and feel supported.
 
After my recent induction into the BGCA Alumni Hall of Fame, this investment feels even more personal to me.
 
This is the kind of impact worth showing up for.
