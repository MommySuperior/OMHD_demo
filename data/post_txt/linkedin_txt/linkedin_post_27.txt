Too often, conversations about AI in mental health focus only on Western contexts, so it was a pleasure to speak with Kat Lay for this article about the development of therapy chatbots for use in Uganda. Some key takeaways from me:

⭐ AI offers the chance to reimagine mental health care—not just replicate existing models. Entirely new approaches could prove as effective, or even better.

⭐  We need to be as ambitious and innovative when developing new regulatory frameworks as we are when developing new tech. We know the science for AI in mental health is already here – getting people access to safe and effective tools will be a challenge for regulators.

⭐ Input from people with lived experience is critical in ensuring any new tools are appropriate and useful for the populations they are meant to help.
