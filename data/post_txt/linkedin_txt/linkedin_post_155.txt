May is Mental Health Awareness Month, and it feels especially important to consider how technology, and AI in particular, can positively impact our mental health in the workplace. 

When we Cisco talk about "AI for Good" we’re referring to technology benefits such as:

* Reducing administrative burnout so employees can spend more time on what truly matters: human connection. 
* Helping leaders identify patterns of workplace exhaustion, enabling us to foster more supportive and inclusive environments.

And while some fear AI has the potential to isolate workers, one study shows that “AI Power Users” spend less time on individual tasks which allows more time for socializing and learning with colleagues compared to non-users (Gensler Global Workplace Survey 2026). 

I’m excited by the potential of these tools to enhance well-being and grateful to be part of a company like Cisco and the larger community committed to this cause.
