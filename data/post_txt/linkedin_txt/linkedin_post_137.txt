Learn how expanded adoption of health IT can improve mental health and substance use care outcomes at the Office of the National Coordinator for Health IT's upcoming event, “Technology’s Role in Making America Healthy Again: A Discussion on Mental Health & Substance Use Care.”

Meadows Institute Deputy Chief Medical Officer Michelle Durham, MD, MPH, DFAPA, DFAACAP will participate in "Roundtable 3: Building the Bridge: From Today's Barriers to Tomorrow's System," where panelists will discuss barriers for Electronic Health Record (EHR) adoption in behavioral health settings and what it will take to fully integrate EHRs into behavioral health care. 

The conversation takes place Monday, April 20, at 1:00 PM CT. No registration is required, and you can watch the livestream here: https://www.hhs.gov/live/
