The new Mental Health Strategy is a welcome commitment from the government, particularly its focus on a shift to prevention by supporting people earlier and faster.

Alongside this, seeing the sector coming together through initiatives like Future Minds and the Head On campaign creates a real opportunity to drive a step change in early intervention across the UK.

As a funder, Wellcome Trust sees first-hand the breakthroughs transforming mental health science. The priority now must be ensuring these innovations are translated into practice, so people can access treatments that work for them. 

This strategy can build on decades of work by clinicians, researchers, advocates and people with lived experience to deliver meaningful progress, so we can achieve a future where no one is held back by mental health problems.
