🚨New publication alert! "Mental health related stigma in a primary care setting in Karnataka, rural India: Service user and carer perspectives"

🌍 Mental health is a global issue, but #stigma and barriers to care are often deeply shaped by local contexts.

Our new qualitative study in PLOS ONE explores the experiences of people living with mental health conditions and their carers in rural Karnataka, India.

The findings highlight four interconnected themes, underscoring the importance of designing locally tailored, culturally sensitive anti-stigma interventions, especially in low- and middle-income settings.

1. Complex challenges in accessing care
2. Gaps in knowledge and awareness
3. The impact of stigma and discrimination
4. The vital role of family and community cohesion
