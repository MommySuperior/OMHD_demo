As Mental Health Awareness Month comes to a close, it is vital to remember that supporting the well-being of young people is a 365-day commitment, requiring continuous attention, collaboration, and action.

To retain a focus on topics related to teen safety and well-being throughout the year, Snapchat has launched “Digital Wellness Check.” This new video series brings together prominent global experts, industry leaders, and other special guests to discuss how we can collaborate to build digital spaces where young people can truly flourish.

For our inaugural episode, I sat down with world-renowned authority on suicide prevention Dan Reidenberg to discuss the whole picture of a teen’s life and how we might shift public dialogue away from fear-based rhetoric toward effective, reality-based approaches.

True digital wellness requires an ecosystem of responsibility. Thank you, Dr. Dan, for sharing your invaluable insights and for your continuous partnership in helping to keep our community safe.
