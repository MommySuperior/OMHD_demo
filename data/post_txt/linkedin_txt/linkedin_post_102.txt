May is Mental Health Awareness Month, a meaningful reminder of the millions of people around the world living with mental health conditions, and of the ongoing need for new approaches to care.

At AbbVie, our commitment to neuroscience is deeply rooted in the belief that understanding the complexities of the brain requires sustained research, strong collaboration and fresh thinking across discovery and development. 

I am encouraged by the recent progress and milestones across our neuroscience pipeline and by the partnerships that are helping us explore promising new pathways and advance potential treatment options. Behind every scientific advancement are patients and families hoping for better options and better outcomes.
