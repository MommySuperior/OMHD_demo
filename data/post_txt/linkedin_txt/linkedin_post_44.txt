Building reliable mental health care at scale is ultimately a technology challenge.

Too many systems today aren’t designed to support continuity, adapt as needs change, or earn trust over time. Bringing Spring Health and Alma  together gives us the opportunity to build a more resilient technical foundation that supports clinicians, protects patient experience, and holds up as care becomes more complex.

I’m excited about what these two teams can build together and the role thoughtful, well-designed technology can play in making mental health care more dependable for everyone who relies on it.
