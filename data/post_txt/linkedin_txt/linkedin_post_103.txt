Truly enjoyed being a part of this from behind the scenes. Two strong organizations that have created measurable impact for decades coming together.

I know this may mark the end of my time on the national board for AFSP, but the impact being created here is far greater than any one role. Bringing together two of the largest mental health organizations—representing over $75 million in combined annual operating budgets has the potential to drive meaningful, measurable change at scale.

Lessons learned: we go bigger and faster together. If someone or something can strengthen where you have gaps, be open to it. This partnership combines AFSP’s leadership in suicide research and prevention with the JED Foundation’s strength in supporting youth, creating a more complete and powerful approach to mental health.

A meaningful step forward for the future of our youth, suicide prevention, and the future of this organization. TOGETHER.
