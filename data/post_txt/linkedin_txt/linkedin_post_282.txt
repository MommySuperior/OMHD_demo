Last weekend, we kicked off Mental Health Month with a powerful reminder of why this work matters so deeply. Nationwide Children's Hospital proudly founded The Kids Mental Health Foundation and our teams were honored to join communities in Columbus and Los Angeles for a series of events elevating one message: children’s mental health starts with each of us.
 
In Columbus, we celebrated Columbus Crew Title Night, where our remarkable patient champions proudly delivered the ceremonial match ball.
 
In Los Angeles, a thought‑provoking panel hosted by Hollywood and Mind to demonstrate how storytelling can change the national conversation around kids’ mental health. We also gathered for an event to honor champions whose advocacy is driving meaningful progress for children and families. Their leadership inspires all of us.
 
And at Kids Mental Health Day at The Grove, families experienced a full day of activities designed to show that mental health is a vital part of every child’s upbringing.
 
As we move through Mental Health Month, I’m grateful for every partner, advocate and family who stands with us. Together, we are building a future where kids’ mental health is understood, supported and prioritized.
