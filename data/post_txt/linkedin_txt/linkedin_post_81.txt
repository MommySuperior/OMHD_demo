What if mental health care is missing a big piece of the puzzle?

In our latest conversation on the Metabolic Mind podcast, Meru Health Founder & CEO Kristian Ranta shares the story behind Meru Health and why he believes we need to pay more attention to metabolic health when treating mental health conditions.

For decades, psychiatry has focused largely on symptoms and medications. But what if factors like nutrition, blood sugar regulation, inflammation, and overall metabolic health are playing a much bigger role than we've traditionally recognized?

That question has been central to Kristian's journey and helped shape the development of Meru Health Advanced, a new program designed for people with treatment-resistant mental health conditions that brings together mental health care, nutrition, and metabolic health support.

As our understanding of mental health evolves, it's becoming clear that we can't separate the brain from the rest of the body.
