Earlier this month, at the National Digital Mental Health Conference, we formally launched PROACT, a new Health Research Board (HRB)  funded partnership between Jigsaw - The National Centre for Youth Mental Health and University of Limerick. PROACT is the first study of its kind in Ireland to examine how data analytics can be applied within youth mental health services, using clinical service data to generate actionable, real-time insights. We aim to equip clinicians with real-time insights that support safer and more effective care for young people across Ireland.
