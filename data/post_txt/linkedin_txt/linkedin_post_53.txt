We believe better mental health support is possible. That's why we've joined 20+ mental health charities to launch the Head On - a campaign to turn public consensus on mental health into political action. We want to go beyond changing conversations and change lives.

Together, we can build a modern mental health system. One where everyone gets the right support, at the right time. This starts by giving mental health the political focus it deserves. 

“Head On brings together over 20 charities who are backed by public opinion in demanding mental health gets the political attention it deserves. We must fund and scale what works – with a focus on driving down mental health waiting lists and prioritising prevention, early intervention and community care. Action can’t wait, and we urgently need a clear, ambitious, strategic vision for our nation’s mental health from the Government.” 
– Andy Bell
