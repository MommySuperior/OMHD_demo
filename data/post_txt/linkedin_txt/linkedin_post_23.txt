Mental health is HEALTH.

The phrase “mental health” is often treated as negative, but everyone has mental health. It is one part of overall health, just like physical health.

We understand that physical injuries, strain, and recovery are part of life. 

Mental and emotional struggles should not be treated as shameful, hidden, or separate from the rest of a person’s wellbeing.'

Carry the Bag is a Luke Cyr Foundation campaign inspired by Luke Cyr’s caddying journey. The bag is a symbol for what people carry, the support they may need, and the importance of connection, purpose, and community.

Participation does not require physically carrying a bag.

People, teams, workplaces, and community partners can take part by sharing the message, supporting someone else, donating, sponsoring, or helping spread awareness.

Everyone has mental health.
Everyone carries something.
