Mental health is a critical part of health and well-being and a fundamental human right. However, mental health conditions affect close to 150 million people in the region and many do not access the support and help they need. 

To support this area of health, the African Region is guided by the Regional framework to strengthen the implementation of the comprehensive mental health action plan 2013–2030 in the African Region. 

Join us for the the first webinar of the 2026 African Region Mental Health Webinar Series focusing on Accelerating Progress on the Regional Mental Health Framework in the African Region as we:

- discuss progress made so far on implementing the framework

- listen to inspiring country experiences 

- consider strategies on accelerating mental health policy and legislative reform, advocating for greater investment in mental health, transformation of mental health services and strengthening mental health data in the region
