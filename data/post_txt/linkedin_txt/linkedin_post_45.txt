Seven years of FDA-grade mental health AI just became open source, and it's worth some attention.

I've followed Grace Chang and the Kintsugi team from afar as they built voice biomarker models that detect depression and anxiety, navigated the FDA De Novo path, and poured $30M into the work. Kintsugi is unfortunately shutting down, but Grace made the rare decision to open-source the core models, methodologies, and research rather than let it sit on a shelf.

If you're building in mental health, voice AI, or digital diagnostics, go look at this.
