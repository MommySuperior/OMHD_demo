New York City Mayor Eric Adams and NYC Mayor's Office of Community Mental Health’s (OCMH) Executive Director Eva Wong released the city’s first-ever Involuntary Transports Dashboard, offering the public unprecedented access to data on involuntary transports for hospital evaluation of individuals in mental health crisis.
 
“This launch reflects our unwavering commitment to ensure those in crisis receive the care they deserve while keeping public spaces safe,” said OCMH Executive Director Eva Wong. “It underscores the City’s focus on transparency, accountability, and coordinated response to urgent needs.”

The dashboard affirms NYC’s dual commitment to compassionate care and public safety — both essential to community well-being.
