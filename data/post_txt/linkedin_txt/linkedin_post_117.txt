Every child deserves to feel seen, heard and supported. Children’s Mental Health Acceptance Day is a reminder that mental health is part of overall health, and that families, caregivers and communities all play a role in creating spaces where young people can thrive.

This May, Oregon Family Support Network will host two gatherings to bring that spirit of acceptance to life. Each gathering is shaped by input from youth and families and offers opportunities to connect, participate in activities and access resources that support families. 

For many families, the journey through mental and behavioral health systems can feel overwhelming. Moments of connection, like these events, create space for relief, understanding and hope.

They remind families:
•	You are not alone
•	Your voice matters
•	Support is available
