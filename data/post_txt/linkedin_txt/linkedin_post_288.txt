YouTube has recently launched dedicated Teen Mental Health Shelves across the UK. 😊

This new update creates a curated space where teenagers can access evidence-based mental health and wellbeing content designed specifically for them. We’ve partnered with leading specialists, including the NHS and Mind to ensure young people find trusted, age-appropriate information where they’re already searching or support.

As Department of Health and Social Care Minister Baroness Merron noted: “YouTube is a vital platform for reaching and educating young people and we're delighted to work with them to share accurate, life-saving information about substance abuse and mental health.” 

Supporting teen mental health is a responsibility we take seriously, and we are committed to creating safer, more supportive online spaces for the next generation. Collaborations like this can make a real difference in young people’s lives through digital content. Huge thanks for all our partners and Dr. Vishaal Virani, MBBS!
