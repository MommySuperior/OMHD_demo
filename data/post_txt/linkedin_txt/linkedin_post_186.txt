Mental health needs aren’t slowing down.

New data shows:
• 48% of Americans say they’re more anxious than last year*
• 62% have talked with a mental health professional at some point**
• Only 27% are currently in therapy**

Remember: checking in with your mental health matters just as much as checking in with your physical health. Mental health care doesn’t have to wait for a crisis. Support can look different for everyone, and asking for help is a sign of strength.

Your primary care provider can help you take the first step and connect you with the support you need.

**Thriveworks, Pulse on Mental Health Report, 2026.
