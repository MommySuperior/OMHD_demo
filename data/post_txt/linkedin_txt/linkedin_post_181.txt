🌍🧠 This #WorldMentalHealthDay, tune in to Holly Coole and Grace Gatera on the Digital Health Unplugged podcast as they explore how AI and digital mental health technologies are being developed and regulated to keep people safe . 

🎧 Holly, a registered mental health nurse and senior manager for digital mental health at the MHRAshares insight into the MHRA’s three-year project, funded by Wellcome Trust, to strengthen regulation and embed lived experience in every stage of digital mental health innovation. 

🌍 Grace, a dedicated mental health lived experience advocate, brings a powerful global perspective from Rwanda, highlighting why the voices of those with lived experience are vital to shaping safer, more inclusive mental health tech. 

💭 “We’re working to ensure digital mental health technologies are safe, effective, and informed by lived experience.” said Holly
