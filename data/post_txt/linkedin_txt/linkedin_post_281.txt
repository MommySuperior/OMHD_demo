The Mental Health Innovation Network (MHIN) Asia Hub, together with the ISC Mental Health and Psychosocial Support (MHPSS) Learning Collaborative at the Center for Innovation in Mental Health (CIMH), are pleased to host this webinar on the implementation of the WHO Comprehensive Mental Health Action Plan 2013–2030 in the Western Pacific (WPRO) and South-East Asia (SEARO) regions.

The session will feature regional and country-level perspectives, including contributions from WHO SEARO and WHO WPRO, highlighting progress, innovations, challenges, and emerging opportunities across diverse contexts. Speakers will share practical examples of implementation strategies, tools, and mechanisms that have strengthened mental health systems, and will offer insights to foster an open and interactive dialogue.

The webinar aims to create a dynamic space for regional exchange and peer learning, and will examine how partnerships - including networks and coalitions such as the MHPSS Learning Collaborative and MHIN - can accelerate implementation and support more coordinated and effective mental health policy and service development.
