We welcome the Department for Science, Innovation and Technology's new Mental Health Goals programme, which will invest up to £50 million in mental health research, and their Lived Experience Industry Partnership, which aims to ensure people with lived experience shape how this research is prioritised.
 
We are pleased to see that Government are supporting industry to connect with patients and communities. This is key to building trust and delivering the research people want and need. As we heard at our recent Northern Ireland Cross-Sector hub event with Health Innovation Research Alliance Northern Ireland (HIRANI) on 'Communities, Mental Health and Technology', lived experience voices are vital to research. 
 
Patient and public involvement, and mental health, are core pillars of the Academy's vision of good health for all, supported by the best research and evidence.
