Last week I attended ViVE 2025 in Nashville with Ebenezer Eyeson-Annan. Artificial Intelligence (AI) took center stage as a game-changer for health care—from ambient AI for note-taking to sophisticated analytics for diagnostics and virtual care. 

I was thrilled to be there showcasing our work in mental health.... 'Mia—the Mental Health Intelligence Agent'—developed in collaboration between Brain and Mind Centre and UNCAPT. Mia is an agentic-AI system designed to support specialised assessment, care planning, and coordination, embedding decades of mental health expertise into its knowledge base.

Mia’s potential? To help scale clinical capacity, alleviate workforce constraints, and ensure consistent, expert-level care for more people—regardless of where they enter the health system. At a time when demand for mental health support is soaring, this kind of innovation is both urgent and achievable right now.
