World Mental Health Day was observed on October 10, 2024. On this day, the Union Health Ministry celebrated the success of the National Tele Mental Health Programme, Tele MANAS, which has completed two years and handled over 1.45 million calls. The Tele MANAS toll-free helpline,
-800-891-4416, offers multilingual support in 20 languages and has been instrumental in connecting individuals with mental health professionals.

At the commemoration event in New Delhi, Smt. Aradhana Patnaik, Additional Secretary of MoHFW and Managing Director of the National Health Mission (NHM, launched the Tele MANAS mobile app and video call facility.

IIIT-Bangalore is proud of its role in developing the Tele MANAS mobile app, which offers free and confidential 24/7 mental health support across India. Developed in-house with guidance from the Tele MANAS Apex Centre at NIMHANS, the app is available on the Google Play Store, with an iOS version coming soon. It includes features such as 24/7 support, an extensive knowledge base, and a user-friendly interface.

Video consultations represent a significant upgrade to the existing audio service, allowing mental health professionals to provide a more personalized experience. This service will initially launch in Karnataka, Jammu & Kashmir, and Tamil Nadu, with plans for nationwide expansion.
